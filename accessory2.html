High-Performance Gaming Mouse

Razer DeathAdder V2

Features: 20,000 DPI optical sensor, ergonomic design, 8 programmable buttons, and Razer Chroma RGB lighting.

Pros: High precision, comfortable for long gaming sessions, customizable buttons, and vibrant RGB lighting.

Cons: Slightly heavier than some other gaming mice, higher price point.

Logitech G Pro X Superlight

Features: Hero 25K sensor, ultra-lightweight design, 5 programmable buttons, and wireless connectivity.

Pros: Extremely lightweight, excellent sensor performance, long battery life, and wireless convenience.

Cons: Limited RGB lighting options, higher price point.

SteelSeries Aerox 3 Wireless

Features: TrueMove Air sensor, ultra-lightweight design, 6 programmable buttons, and RGB lighting.

Pros: Very lightweight, excellent sensor performance, customizable buttons, and vibrant RGB lighting.

Cons: Battery life could be better, higher price point.
