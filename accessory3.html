RGB Mechanical Keyboard

Corsair K95 RGB Platinum

Features: Cherry MX Speed mechanical switches, dynamic RGB backlighting, 6 programmable macro keys, and a detachable wrist rest.

Pros: High-quality build, excellent typing experience, vibrant RGB lighting, and customizable macros.

Cons: Higher price point, software can be complex for beginners.

Razer BlackWidow Elite

Features: Razer Green mechanical switches, Chroma RGB lighting, dedicated media controls, and a magnetic wrist rest.

Pros: Durable build, tactile and responsive switches, vibrant RGB lighting, and convenient media controls.

Cons: Higher price point, wrist rest could be more comfortable.

Logitech G910 Orion Spectrum

Features: Romer-G mechanical switches, customizable RGB lighting, 9 programmable macro keys, and integrated ARX Control app.

Pros: Excellent build quality, responsive switches, vibrant RGB lighting, and customizable macros.

Cons: Higher price point, software can be complex for beginners.
